Types of Captcha

  • Text CAPTCHAs
  • Image CAPTCHA
  • Audio CAPTCHA
  • Math or verbal problems

Pro and cons of Text Captcha

Pro of textCaptcha

  • Protection against bots and spam
  • Improved security
  • Easy implementation
  • Accessible
  • Easy to use

Cons of textCaptcha

  • Potential inaccessibility
  • Annoying
  • Ability to bypass
  • Causes slowdowns
  • Not suitable for all situations
